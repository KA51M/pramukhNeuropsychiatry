Psychological & Counseling Services

In-depth counseling and standard psychotherapeutic evaluations conducted by our trained clinical team.

Psychotherapy Programs

  • check_circle Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Correcting negative thought loops to cure anxiety and depression.
  • check_circle REBT (Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy): Overcoming irrational beliefs and emotional disturbances.
  • check_circle Relationship & Couple Therapy: Healing communication gaps and building marital understanding.
  • check_circle Family Therapy: Supportive guidance sessions for de-addiction recovery and child mental health.

Clinical Psychometry & Testing

  • check_circle Intelligence (IQ) Testing: Child IQ testing using verified scales (Binet-Kamat, WISC) for learning support.
  • check_circle Personality Assessments: Standardized diagnostic tests for clinical evaluations.
  • check_circle Developmental Evaluations: Assessments for ADHD, Autism, and learning disabilities.
